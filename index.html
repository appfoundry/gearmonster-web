GearMonster

Android app for stompbox aficionados.

What is it?

GearMonster is a pet project over at AppFoundry - a software development team based in Kontich, Belgium. It allows you to input your stompbox collection, group effect pedals (by pedalboard for example), add pictures, keep a wantlist, add notes, etc.
